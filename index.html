ABOUT TRILIVE

Presenting Trilive, A Tribeca Venture, A first of its kind managed rental living concept in India bridging the gap of the highly underserved quality rental housing segment. Estimated to grow $13.9Bn within the next 5 years, this is a smart, future-ready investment for savvy investors. Professionally managed by Housr, Trilive will offer built-to-suit high quality studios offering top-tier service and hassle free maintenance

ABOUT TRIBECA

Founded in 2012, Tribeca is India’s leading design focused real estate developer and committed to crafting iconic landmarks that redefine cityscapes.

With each project our simple goal is to make it the most prestigious property in its market – a “Trophy Property”. We benchmark our projects against the world’s best and are the largest developer of Trump branded residences in the world.

ABOUT HOUSR

Housr, known as the business class of coliving, is India's top luxury-managed accommodation provider, offering a living experience that is par excellence. Exclusively focused on working professionals and the millennial generation, Housr provides a comfortable, connected and curated way of living where one can enjoy all the modern amenities under one roof and an enriching community experience. At Housr, you can enjoy a completely hassle-free life with an array of incredible amenities along with top-notch lifestyle services.
